### Research Interests

A phylogenetic tree is a pivotal framework for solving fundamental issues in biology.
My long-term research goal is to use a robust understanding of phylogeny to elucidate
patterns of evolutionary radiations in angiosperms and to address the causes of plant
diversification using the tools of comparative biology.
